# myPangea

React App for international travelers who wish to keep a digital journal / photo album. This app allows you to document your experiences and explore past trips through a map filtering device. Users can add and delete entries and may only interact with application after being properly authenticated through js web token. Back end/database written with Express framework 

<img width="950" alt="mypangea" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/91293977/197660008-fb0d2dc9-0ede-4b06-99cf-1598493b59dd.png">

Above is the main screen. Users can create an account through the registration page and after an account is created, they will be brought to the log in screen. After user is properly authenticated, they will be redirected to their myPangea page which includes an interactive/clickable map and a list of all of their entries.

Users are able to click on their entries to view the location, image, time and origin of upload. They are also able to delete their entry with the delete button. See below.

<img width="960" alt="pangeaentry" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/91293977/197660474-49f773fa-5e0f-4d48-91b3-0c507704d6b8.png">

Users can upload their own entries which will then be added to their list of myPangea entries to browse through with a clickable map to sort entry by continent.
